About Us

Welcome to Masterton Roofing Limited! With years of experience in the industry, our team is dedicated to delivering top-quality roofing solutions that stand the test of time. We take pride in our craftsmanship, attention to detail, and commitment to customer satisfaction. From the initial consultation to the final inspection, we ensure every project is completed efficiently, safely, and to the maximum standards. Trust us to provide you with a roof that not only looks great but also offers lasting protection for your home or business.


Services

We specialize in advanced single-ply roofing solutions, including the installation of PVC membrane systems, which come with a minimum 20-year warranty. This high-performance material offers exceptional durability, weather resistance, and energy efficiency for your property. Our team expertly installs complete build-ups from the deck upwards, ensuring a long-lasting roofing system designed by us, tailored to your specific needs. Whether it's a new build or a refurbishment, you can rely on us for professional single ply roofing services that deliver peace of mind.
